Pricing policy

Price promise information

John Lewis says it checks prices against 25 main competitors and can refund the difference if an eligible item bought from John Lewis is found cheaper within 7 days.

  • Matched item must be identical and in stock for delivery.
  • The policy covers items sold directly by named competitors, not marketplace or third-party sellers.
  • Some offer types are excluded, including clearance, multi-buy, member-only, flash sale and promo-code prices.
  • John Lewis also says it matches its own lower prices up to 7 days after purchase.

Guarantees

TV guarantee information

John Lewis states that TVs include a 5 year guarantee at no extra cost. Its guarantee page also points customers to product category information, exclusions, support options and Protect Plus accidental damage insurance.

  • Minimum 2 year guarantee on electricals.
  • 5 year guarantee on TVs.
  • Manufacturer-accredited repairers are referenced by John Lewis.
