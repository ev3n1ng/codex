Apricity

uh-PRIS-i-tee

Description
The warmth of sunlight in winter; a small, bright comfort on a cold day.
Usage
We sat by the window, borrowing a little apricity from the pale afternoon.
Etymology
Latin - apricitas, warmth of the sun - apricus, sunny or sunlit - English apricity.
